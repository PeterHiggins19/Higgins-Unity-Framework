# Loudspeaker Analogy — Where HUF Began

Loudspeaker crossover networks are compositional systems: power split across drivers sums to 1. The PLL discipline (6 locked rules) originates from BTL loudspeaker engineering constraints. The 6.02 dB budget, 115 Hz·m, and 5.5-octave bandwidth are locked constants.

This is the origin story — the realization that audio crossover engineering IS compositional data analysis.
