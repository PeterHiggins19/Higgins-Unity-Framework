# Gifts to Every Community

## What the Framework Brings to Each Field — and What It Asks in Return

**Status:** This is not a claim. It is an inventory of what the work offers.

---

## To the CoDa Community

**The gift:** Empirical proof that the geometric mean preserves information. 40 years of theoretical argument now has a number: 1.8% vs 14.2%. Aitchison was right. Here is the receipt.

**What it asks:** Nothing. The framework uses CoDa's own tools, CoDa's own geometry, CoDa's own language. It extends, it does not replace.

## To Energy Agencies and Policy Makers

**The gift:** MC-4 composition monitoring. Detect when an energy mix changed, which carrier moved, whether the change was real or noise. Self-calibrated per country. Catches Fukushima, Germany's nuclear exit, UK's coal collapse — all without being told they happened.

**What it asks:** Replace arithmetic averaging with geometric averaging on compositional data. One line of code.

## To Nuclear Physics

**The gift:** A compositional lens on the valley of stability. The sigma_A^2 heat map (light nuclei hot, actinides cold). The decay chain PLL signatures (relaxation toward stability). The SEMF boundary species (Volume binding as the dominant force).

**What it asks:** Nothing. The framework does not modify SEMF or shell theory. It reads what was already there through a different lens.

## To Geochemistry

**The gift:** EITT validation on 40,666 real rocks (37/39 TAS types pass). A bridge between CoDa's birthplace (Aitchison worked in geochemistry) and temporal monitoring. Foidite correctly identified as the outlier.

**What it asks:** Apply CoDa methods to time-ordered geochemical data — differentiation sequences, eruption histories, metamorphic grade progressions.

## To Metrologists

**The gift:** An instrument with its own operating envelope. Documented precision bounds. Safety fences. F17 early warning. The two-pass architecture separates science from integrity. The method comes with its own user manual for failure.

**What it asks:** Evaluate the instrument by metrology standards. Report where the envelope is wrong.

## To Statisticians

**The gift:** The Vertex Theorem. Exact mathematics. Chain rule identity. The clr orthogonal to clr' orthogonality condition at the vertex of sigma_A^2 — a pure geometric fact about compositional time series that was sitting there waiting to be stated.

**What it asks:** Formalize it. Generalize it. Find the conditions under which sigma_A^2 is generically parabolic. Prove or disprove the conjecture.

## To PLL / Control Engineers

**The gift:** 30 domains where the parabolic lock geometry appears. The engineering instinct — tune to the millivolt minimum — works on gold prices, stellar nucleosynthesis, wine fermentation, room acoustics, and particle physics. The screwdriver works everywhere.

**What it asks:** Apply PLL-specific predictions (capture range, pull-in time, bandwidth) to compositional data. See if the analogy is quantitative, not just qualitative.

## To Boundary-State Communities

**The gift:** The failures are diagnostic. West Virginia fails EITT because there IS no energy transition — coal won decades ago. Foidite fails because deep mantle chaos destroys temporal autocorrelation. Rhode Island fails because it is gas-dominated with 47% zeros. Every failure tells you something that percentage monitoring cannot.

**What it asks:** Nothing. The diagnosis is free.

## To Sceptics

**The gift:** The Claim Classification. Every claim has its promotion level, its evidence, its demotion conditions, and its contamination risk. The Bell test is flagged VERY HIGH risk. The PLL analogy is labeled "ahead of proof." The nuclear corridor is "corridor-level only." The framework tells you how to attack it.

**What it asks:** Attack it. Find the defeats. The framework was built to survive legitimate attacks and fall to legitimate critiques.

## To the World

**The gift:** The simplex was always there. Wherever something is divided into parts that must sum to a whole — energy carriers, chemical elements, species abundances, financial portfolios, particle yields — the geometry exists. The compositions have their own space, their own mean, their own distance, their own dynamics. This framework makes those dynamics readable.

**What it asks:** Use it. Break it where it breaks. Fix what is fixable. Leave what is not.
