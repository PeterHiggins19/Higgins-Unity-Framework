# Coimbra Welcome Package — HUF for CoDaWork 2026

**Prepared:** 2026-04-15
**For:** CoDaWork 2026 attendees, CoDa researchers, and anyone arriving at the Higgins Unity Framework for the first time.

## What this package is

A self-contained introduction to HUF as a working tool. You can read it straight through in roughly 45 minutes. By the end you should know what HUF is, where it works and where it doesn't, how to use it, how to verify its claims, what's stable and what's still being built, who helps you when you get stuck, and what you get for adopting it.

## The one-sentence version

> HUF-GOV is a tool that produces a verifiably clean usable output to a known degree of certainty; the diagnostic of the output is open to interpretation by expert decision and open to modification by expert judgment.

If you remember nothing else, remember that sentence. Everything else in this package is an unpacking of it.

## The founding principle

The past is our strength and our future.

HUF's engineering DNA is Rogue Wave Audio's DADC-DADI-ADAC loudspeaker diffraction correction chain (December 2024 forward). The compositional mathematics was discovered empirically in acoustic engineering before it was formalized. The two public repos — [Higgins Unity Framework](https://github.com/PeterHiggins19/Higgins-Unity-Framework) and [Rogue Wave Audio](https://github.com/PeterHiggins19/Rogue-Wave-Audio) — are siblings, and both are open.

Welcome aboard. Bon voyage.
