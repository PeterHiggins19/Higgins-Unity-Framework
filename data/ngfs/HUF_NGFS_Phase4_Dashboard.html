Key Findings

Net Zero 2050 shows highest governance efficiency

REMIND-MAgPIE Net Zero 2050 achieves GovEff = 1.83 — the steadiest, most purposeful compositional transition. Solar rises from 0.6% to 51.8%, coal drops from 28% to 0.25%.

Current Policies = compositional stagnation

GCAM Current Policies shows GovEff = 0.15 — energy mix barely moves on the simplex. The world drifts, the composition stagnates. K1 distance only 3.4 vs 9.6 for Net Zero.

MESSAGEix regions show wildly different transition paths

Middle East/North Africa under Current Policies has GovEff = 6.18 (high χ̄ but high W = 10.3 — lots of wandering). Regions that transition big also wander the most.

Winding ratio distinguishes policy quality

Net Zero W ≈ 5.0 (moderate winding) vs Delayed Transition W ≈ 5.2 (more reversals). The delay creates compositional backtracking — detectable on the simplex before it shows in emissions.

Methodology

HUF Compositional Diagnostics on IIASA Energy Data

Source: NGFS Phase 4 V4.0, IAM_data.xlsx — 496,224 rows of scenario projections from three Integrated Assessment Models (GCAM 6.0, MESSAGEix-GLOBIOM 1.1, REMIND-MAgPIE 3.2) across 7 climate policy scenarios and 62 world regions.

Composition: At each 5-year timestep (2020–2100), primary energy is decomposed into 9 parts: Coal, Gas, Oil, Nuclear, Hydro, Solar, Wind, Biomass, Geothermal. Each row is closed to the simplex (proportions summing to 1).

CLR Transform: Centered log-ratio transform maps compositions from the simplex to unconstrained Euclidean space, where the Aitchison metric applies.

χ̄ (Crossing Intensity): Average Aitchison distance between consecutive timesteps. Measures how fast the energy mix is changing.

CV(χ) (Uniformity): Coefficient of variation of the per-step crossing intensities. Low CV = steady transition pace; high CV = jerky/uneven.

W (Winding Ratio): Total simplex path length divided by direct geodesic distance. W = 1.0 means perfectly straight; W > 5 means significant wandering or policy reversals.

Governance Efficiency = χ̄ / (1 + CV(χ)): Rewards fast, steady transitions. Penalizes stop-start or wandering paths.

K1 Distance: Aitchison distance between the 2020 and 2100 compositions — total transformation achieved on the simplex.

Framework: Higgins Unity Framework (HUF) — compositional monitoring layer complementary to optimization models. HUF does not tell MESSAGEix or REMIND what to optimize; it diagnoses the compositional quality of whatever path emerges.
