## Three Structural Issues Identified

### Issue 1: Wave-Mechanics Language Is Too Hot

The evidence is real but the verbs cross the line into theorem-claiming. Specific phrases flagged:

- "IS isomorphic" — too strong without formal proof
- "IS the filter bank" — conflates observation with theorem
- "IS the coherence residual" — same pattern

**Copilot's recommendation:** Keep the reframing. Dial down the verbs. Move loudspeaker bridge to second room.

**Recommended replacements:**

- "empirically behaves as..."
- "mathematically equivalent under the present formulation..."
- "supports the interpretation that..."

### Issue 2: THE_INSTRUMENT.md Merge Is Too Aggressive

The 27-to-10 consolidation is correct but merging four documents into one risks losing conceptual scaffolding. Copilot proposes splitting into two:

1. **THE_INSTRUMENT.md** — What the instrument is, what the diagnostics do, what the evidence shows
2. **THE_LINEAGE_AND_BRIDGE.md** — Loudspeaker origin, wave-mechanics reframing, SBP filter-bank equivalence, why the engineering intuition mattered

This preserves clarity and reduces proof burden.

### Issue 3: S016 Evidence Isn't in the Repo

The strongest quantitative outputs live outside the repo in Claude CoWorker. This is a governance and credibility risk.

**Copilot's recommendation:** Promote all JSON files to `data/codawork-samples/S016/`.
